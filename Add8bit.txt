Addition of Two 8 bit numbers

MVI B, 06H       Move immediately data 06H into register B
MOV A, B         Move the contents of register B into accumulator
MVI C, 07H       Move immediately data 07H into register C
ADD C            Add the contents of register C with accumulator
STA 8500H        Store the contents of accumulator into memory address 8500H
HLT              Stop the program execution
